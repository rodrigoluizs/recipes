---
title: Cheese Bread (Pão de Queijo)
servings: 30
prepTime: 40 min
cookTime: 30 min
ingredients:
  - amount: 250
    unit: g
    name: semi-cured cheese
    note: grated
  - amount: 85
    unit: g
    name: parmesan cheese
    note: grated
  - amount: 50
    unit: g
    name: oil
  - amount: 200
    unit: g
    name: milk
  - amount: 250
    unit: g
    name: sour cassava starch
    note: polvilho azedo
  - amount: 85
    unit: g
    name: sweet cassava starch
    note: polvilho doce
  - amount: 6
    unit: g
    name: salt
  - amount: 1
    name: whole egg
method:
  - In a pot, heat the [[milk]], [[oil]] and [[salt]] until it starts to boil.
  - Place the [[sour cassava starch]] and [[sweet cassava starch]] in a large bowl and pour the hot mixture on top, mixing well to scald.
  - Let it cool until lukewarm, then mix in the [[whole egg]] until fully absorbed.
  - Add the [[semi-cured cheese]] and [[parmesan cheese]] and knead until you get a smooth, uniform dough.
  - Shape the dough into small balls and place them on a lined baking tray.
  - Bake at 200°C for about 30 minutes, until golden and puffed.
tags:
  - brazilian
  - cheese
  - snack
---

Best served warm. The dough can be shaped and frozen raw — bake straight from
frozen, adding a few extra minutes.
