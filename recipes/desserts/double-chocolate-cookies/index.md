ingredients:
  - amount: 200
    unit: g
    name: dark chocolate
    note: chopped
  - amount: 120
    unit: g
    name: butter
  - amount: 150
    unit: g
    name: brown sugar
  - amount: 2
    name: eggs
  - amount: 180
    unit: g
    name: flour
  - amount: 40
    unit: g
    name: cocoa powder
  - amount: 1
    unit: tsp
    name: baking powder
  - amount: 150
    unit: g
    name: chocolate chips
  - name: salt
    note: a pinch
method:
  - Melt the [[dark chocolate]] and [[butter]] together, then let cool slightly.
  - Whisk the [[eggs]] with the [[brown sugar]] until pale and thick.
  - Stir the melted chocolate into the egg mixture.
  - "Sift in the [[flour]], [[cocoa powder]], [[baking powder]] and [[salt]]; fold until just combined."
  - Fold in the [[chocolate chips]], then chill the dough for 1 hour.
  - Scoop onto a lined tray and bake at 180°C for 12 minutes, until the edges set but the centres stay soft.
