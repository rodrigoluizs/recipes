---
title: Fresh Pasta Recipe
servings: 4
prepTime: 22 min
cookTime: 5 min
ingredients:
  - amount: 400
    unit: g
    name: 00 flour
  - amount: 4
    name: eggs
  - amount: 1
    unit: tbsp
    name: olive oil
  - name: salt
    note: to taste
method:
  - Mound the [[00 flour]] on a clean surface and make a well in the centre.
  - Crack the [[eggs]] into the well, add the [[olive oil]] and a pinch of [[salt]].
  - Beat the eggs with a fork, gradually pulling in flour until a shaggy dough forms.
  - Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic, then wrap and rest for 30 minutes.
  - Roll out thinly and cut into your preferred shape.
  - Cook in well-salted boiling water for 2–3 minutes, until tender.
tags:
  - pasta
  - italian
sourceUrl: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pasta
---

Rest is not optional — it relaxes the gluten so the dough rolls out without
springing back.
